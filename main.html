NFL Pick 'Em Rules

  • Each week, select 2 teams.
  • Each team must be selected once, four teams may be selected twice.
  • Grey teams signify they haven't been picked. Yellow teams signify they have been picked once. Red teams signify that they have been picked twice and may not be picked again.
  • If your team wins, you earn the amount of points they win by. If your team loses, you lose the amount of points they lose by.
  • Each week, selection opens at 6am Tuesday morning, and locks at 7pm Thursday night.

Select 2 winners each week and earn your spot at the top of the leaderboard!
